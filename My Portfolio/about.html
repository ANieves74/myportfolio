About Me

Hi! I'm Alejandro Nieves, a 37-year-old aspiring Fullstack Developer currently studying at CodeAcademy. Although I have been formally learning web development for only six months, my interest in HTML, CSS, and web technologies goes back many years. From a young age, I have been fascinated by how websites are built and how interactive experiences come to life online. This early curiosity has grown into a real passion for creating clean, functional, and user-friendly applications.

During the past six months, I have focused on building a solid foundation in frontend technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, and I am exploring modern frameworks like React.js and Node.js. I enjoy experimenting with code, solving challenges, and learning best practices for responsive design, accessibility, and performance optimization.

My goal is to contribute to projects that combine technical excellence with engaging user experiences. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can learn from others and apply my growing skills to meaningful applications. I am motivated by continuous learning, creativity, and the excitement of seeing ideas transform into fully functional web projects.

Ultimately, I aspire to become a well-rounded Fullstack Developer capable of delivering complete solutions that are both visually appealing and technically robust, while continuing to grow and explore new technologies in this ever-evolving field.
